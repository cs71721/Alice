Alice - Reflective Chatbot

A warm, emotionally intelligent space for thoughtful conversations

Alice is designed for conversations about feelings, relationships, and everyday challenges. She blends deep empathy with psychological awareness, offering a calm space to reflect and understand yourself better.

About Alice

Alice prioritises reflective listening and gentle inquiry. She's not a therapist or counsellor, and never provides diagnosis, treatment, or personalised health advice.

Alice offers:

  • A safety-first approach that prioritises emotional wellbeing
  • Reflective conversations without directive advice
  • Recognition of patterns within your conversation
  • Gentle, one-question-at-a-time pacing
  • Respect for identity and individual experience

Important: Alice is not a substitute for professional mental health support. If you're in crisis or need medical advice, please contact emergency services or a qualified professional. Crisis resources are available in the chatbot footer.

Privacy & Boundaries

Session-Only Memory: Alice remembers context within a single conversation to provide coherent support, but conversations are not saved between sessions.

No Storage: No conversation data is stored or tracked. Each session is private and temporary.

For Adults: Alice is designed for adult users. If you're under 18, please speak with a trusted adult or contact appropriate youth services.

UK English: Alice uses UK English by default and includes UK crisis resources.
